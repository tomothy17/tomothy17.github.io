Pennsylvania Lore

1681 April 1

Pennsylvania was founded.

1685 March 10

A small cult forms worshipping bats. The cult is called "Ratpenat", and view the bat species as vessels of an omnipotent God.

1686 January 2

The Ratpenat cult forms a crypt under Pennsylvania where they carry out their ritualistic activities.

1687 January 10

A bat used in one of the cult's rituals starts to glow and becomes deformed. This was the first of its kind, later known as a "Mistuff".

1690 March 7

After a massive influx of bats, several superstitious townspeople saw this as a sign of misfortune, and many innocent people - and some cult members - were burned at the stake. The heart of the crypt began to decay and became unstable. Over time, however, some of its energy dissipated.

1784 March 28

Sir Jonathan Culahan - a wealthy miser - moves to Pennsylvania with REDACTED and commissions a large mansion to be built.

1785 March 28

Drago Culahan, the son of Jonathan and REDACTED, is born.

1790 June 14

At a young age, Drago is fascinated by exploring and finding things outdoors, and develops a passion for adventure.

1810 November 25

Drago drops out of school and becomes an explorer, and starts his adventures in his home state of Pennsylvania.

1813


December 24

Drago discovers a very old crypt, and while exploring through it he accidentally cracks a mysterious bat statue releasing an energy which transforms him; turning his skin pale, sharpening his teeth, an unexplainable hatred for sunlight, and more.


December 25

After the many changes, he is quarantined inside of the Culahan Manor by his father.


December 26

Pennsylvanian locals mock Drago, and begin refering to him as "Dracula".


December 31

A thirst for blood overtakes Drago and he kills his father and REDACTED, leading him into a path of villainy.

1831 - 1901

Pennsylvania splits in two, seperating the Pennsylvanian Slums from the rest of the state. Culahan Manor resides close to the border, overshadowing the slums.

1901 September 4

Myths of "Dracula" living in the Culahan Manor spread throughout the townspeople.

1910


March 16

A vagabond named Jonesy Fnite arrives in Pennsylvania.


March 18

Jonesy breaks into the manor and visciously insults Drago, which pisses him off so much that it hurls Pennsylvania into an brutal ongoing civil war. Due to this violence, an uprising of rebels emerge from the chaos and call themselves the "Bomboclats", or "Bombos" for short. Whenever the Bombos retaliate, Drago attacks tenfold.

1945 August 26

Legendary Jonesy Fnite dies in battle, and his soul now wonders the slums where he still aids the heroic Bombos.

1979 September 1

A raccoon named Ramen ventures to the Pennsylvanian slums.
