Bomboclats


Yonks ago, a group of Pennsylvanian slum dwellers who had names themselves the Bomboclats, commonly refered to as the Bombos, attacked Count Dracula's mansion for an unknown reason.

To this day, Bombos still throw rocks and bricks at Drac's mansion, however whenever the Bombos attack, Dracula retaliate tenfold. Bombos and innocent slum dwellers are still slain in their hundreds, and their slums being destroyed more and more every day.

Maple Cartel


In 1989, a small group of Bomboclat outcasts decided they were tired of the war against Count Dracula so they fled north into Canada. In Canada, they quickly stumbled upon a huge maple forest. As they had been travelling for a long time and were hungry, they tried to eat the leaves and found out they did not taste good. One of them, however, did realise that drying the leaves and smoking them did taste good.

As they headed into the city, Canadians saw them smoking the maple and asked to try it. After they saw everyone enjoying it, they decided to start selling the drug. It quickly became very popular and the CCIA (Canadian Central Intelligence Agency) started to secretly fund their dealing in exchange for a cut of the profit to improve Canada's infrastructure.

The Maple Cartel are insistent on the fact that they are entirely seperate from the Bombos and are peaceful unlike the Bombo's violent nature.

Canada


Canadians can only speak using Canadian stereotypes as words, such as 'ice hockey' and 'poutine'.

Before the 1990s, Canada was only Toronto and empty space everywhere else, with a few maple forests scattered throughout, especially near the American border, until the Maple Cartel began generating stupendous amounts of money which allowed Canada to build more.

In the early 1990s, nearly the entire country was a giant vast city which was just Toronto duplicated. America and the CIA noticed Canada's huge economic growth and did not want to let their status of most powerful country be challenged. They launched a full-scale attack against Canada which caused a decade-long war.

The war ended abruptly in 2002 when the CIA thought Canada was responsible for the 9/11 attacks, although the CCIA denied it, and decimated the entire country using nuclear weapons. Canada is now as it was pre-90s, with Toronto as its only settlement, everywhere else being a wasteland or a maple forest.

Bao


Bao is the brother of Bei and is a dangerous criminal who molests and eats people. He is insanely fat and is to believed to have molested and eaten over 2,000,000,000 (two billion) people.

Whenever he poops it is molten is likely to cause burns if it comes into contact with your skin. He can also cocoon himself in his feces. This does not kill him as he has severe genetic mutation. Evidence suggests he is currently residing in West Korea's Bao Complex.

Bei


Bei is the sister of Bao and is an adult model. Although her and Bao are twins, she is three times younger as she ages 3x slower due to genetic mutation.

West Korea


In 1984, West Korea was founded. It was an incredibly advanced underwater country protected by a dome. The dome is powered by 100 small purple tubes and the island rises for 12 minutes every 12 hours, to ensure its secrecy is kept.

It had a large brutalist concrete building that was intended to be a prison for keeping chomos away from the outside world, until in 1986 a new inmate called Bao ate all the other prisoners and all the guards. Bao is currently using the building as a storage facility for all his victims and calls it the Bao Complex. Authorities refuse to attempt Bao's removal from the island, as he is too dangerous.
